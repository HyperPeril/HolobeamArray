The Olympian Holobeam Array

The Future of Energy

The Olympian Holobeam Array was built to evaluate the Holographic Principle and study the nature of space and time. We are searching for evidence that our three-dimensional universe is a holographic reflection of the two-dimensional surface reality of the cosmological horizon. We are seeking to expand the boundary of physics and humanity's knowledge of the cosmos.
